How it works?

Our College Predictor tool leverages data extracted from official government college eligibility documents and JoSAA records from the previous year (2023). By meticulously analyzing this data, we have developed a robust backend to provide accurate college recommendations. When you input your rank, category, gender, and home state, our algorithm considers India's quota system, which allocates seats based on gender, category, and physical disability. We prioritize matching you with top government colleges in or near your state, ensuring that you receive the best possible recommendations tailored to your specific criteria.
